Overview

Purpose

This site is intended to reach and inform those with questions about the standard works of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It will simply explain Latter-day Saint beliefs about scripture as a whole, as well as further into individual standard works.

Audience

The intended audience will be those unfamiliar with the beliefs and doctrines of the Church, be they nonmembers or newer members looking to learn in more depth regarding the doctrines of the Church.

Home page

Intro and Basics

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints currently recognizes four books of scripture in an open canon: the Holy Bible, the Book of Mormon, the Doctrine and Covenants, and the Pearl of Great Price. Each is a volume of holy writ intended to fill our souls with faith and draw us closer to our Heavenly Father and our Lord Jesus Christ. Each has unique characteristics that warrant extended and individual study, several of which are explained on this site.
